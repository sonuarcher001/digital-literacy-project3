# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Use strong and secure passwords.
- Maintain a professional online profile.
- Think carefully before posting online.
- Respect others while communicating online.
- Verify information before sharing.

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly.
- Do not click suspicious links.
- Avoid spreading fake news.
- Do not post offensive or harmful content.
- Do not share OTPs or sensitive details.
